How to Compare Casino Bonuses

Wagering Requirements Are Everything

The wagering requirement is the number of times you must bet bonus funds before you can withdraw. A 100% match bonus with 40x wagering is materially worse than a 50% match with 15x wagering. Always calculate the total amount you need to wager, not just the bonus percentage.

Free Spins — What to Check

Free spins bonuses give you a set number of spins on designated slots without using your own money. Winnings are typically subject to 20x–40x wagering. Check the RTP of the designated game — higher RTP games offer substantially better expected value from free spins offers.

UK Bonus Regulations

Under UKGC regulations, all bonus terms must be clearly displayed before a player opts in. Casinos must offer a clear opt-out from bonus offers. Wagering requirements must be stated as a multiple of the bonus amount (not deposit plus bonus) unless explicitly stated otherwise.
